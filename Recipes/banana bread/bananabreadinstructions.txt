Blend together butter, sugar, and eggs
Blend together in separate bowl all purpose flour, soda and salt
Add to the butter/egg/sugar mix some of the flour, about 1/2 cup, then stir it all in.
Then add about 1/3 of the bananas, then stir them in.
Repeat until flour and bananana are all added in, then add nuts.
Bake at 350 F. for 20/30 mins, use the knife test to see if finished.
